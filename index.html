UI/UX Design

UI (User Interface) refers to the screens, buttons, toggles, icons, and other visual elements you interact with when using a website, app, or electronic device. It’s about how things look and feel on the surface1. UX (User Experience), on the other hand, encompasses the entire interaction you have with a product. It includes how you feel about that interaction. UX designers focus on making products functional, accessible, and enjoyable to use.
